Dental Appointment Timeline

Appointment Booking

Patients book their appointment online, via phone, or in person. Confirmation and reminders are sent.

Step 1

Patient Check-In

Upon arrival, patients check in, fill out forms (if new), and provide insurance details.

Step 2

Examination & Diagnosis

The dentist examines teeth, gums, and may take X-rays to diagnose any issues.

Step 3

Treatment & Cleaning

Depending on the diagnosis, cleaning, fillings, or other treatments are performed.

Step 4

Billing & Payment

The patient settles the bill and schedules any follow-up appointments if necessary.

Step 5

Departure & Follow-Up

The patient leaves with post-treatment instructions and receives follow-up reminders.
